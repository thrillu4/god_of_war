Will God of War Ragnarök have an upgrade path?

Yes, God of War Ragnarök will offer a digital upgrade* option from a disc or digital PS4 game to the digital PS5 version for an additional cost.

What is god of war about?

First era (Greek mythology) God of War was first released in North America on March 22, 2005, for the PlayStation 2. After ten years in the service of the Olympian gods, Spartan soldier Kratos is tasked by Athena to find Pandora's box, the key to defeating Ares, the God of War, who is running amok through Athens.
